Hmlet

Established in 2016, Hmlet is a tech coliving company that offers rooms and full apartments for rent in the heart of the city. We want to simplify housing for people from all around the world relocating or living in Singapore and Tokyoy.

Motivation

Hmlet caters to international talent, helping them balance their lifestyles, finances and natural desire to connect. At Hmlet we want to revive communal and meaningful interactions in a way that amplifies creativity and empowers our members.

Flexible Lease

Hmlet™ members can rent a room on a month-by-month basis with min. commitment of 3 months.

First time moving in the city? don't want to commit to year long lease? Share a flat in as many locations as you want, and discover awesome flatmates along the way.
